About Rohit

With over a decade of experience in financial consulting and wealth management, Rohit Bornarkar stands as a beacon of excellence in both personal transformation and business optimization. As the Chief Operating Officer and Principal Wealth Advisor at The Financialist, he has democratized private wealth management across India.

Armed with an MBA from the prestigious Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad and holding the distinguished CFP (Certified Financial Planner) and RIA (Registered Investment Advisor) credentials, Rohit brings unparalleled expertise to his dual mission of life coaching and business consultancy.

Through his innovative approaches with Super Life and ExcelMidas, Rohit has transformed hundreds of lives and businesses, creating a legacy of sustainable success and balanced living. His philosophy centers on the belief that true prosperity encompasses both personal fulfillment and professional excellence.
